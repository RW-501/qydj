About QYDJ Events

Quit Your Day Job Events (QYDJ) is not just an event company — it's a movement. We create unforgettable themed experiences that go beyond the party and leave a lasting impression.

Our Story

Born from a dream to break free from the 9-to-5 grind, QYDJ started as a rebellious spark — a desire to bring something bold, immersive, and real to the event scene. Today, we’re turning that dream into curated experiences across cities, seasons, and celebrations.

Whether it's a rooftop party with live DJs or a neon-lit masquerade, every QYDJ event is designed to hit different — because your free time deserves more than “just okay.”

What We Do

  • Themed parties and immersive experiences
  • Pop-ups, rooftop gatherings, and cultural events
  • Collabs with DJs, brands, and local artists
  • Vendor and sponsorship opportunities
  • Creative marketing and content production

Let’s Work Together

Interested in becoming a vendor? Want to sponsor a vibe? Looking to collaborate? We’re always looking for fresh energy and innovative partnerships. Let’s make magic.
